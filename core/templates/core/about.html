About Us

Zlatko and Zoran Trifunovski have been working in the grocery retail business for over 40 years combined. Experiences include, Buying for a major North American retailer, Category Management and National Key Account Management for global tier one consumer packaged goods companies. They are both very well versed in all aspects of the grocery retail industry. Having built a solid reputation around the foundation of integrity and respect, Zlatko and Zoran’s passion for people and commitment to sales growth has been continuously proven in the results of the businesses they have managed over the years. Understanding the needs and trends of Canada’s very dynamic grocery retailers, they have identified a need for a more focused and professional account management service that goes beyond the initial product listing.

Having this well rounded backgroud in the grocery retail industry, Trifuno Sales Inc. and Trifuno Retail Solutions Inc. were founded to combine the value of strong relationships with fact based sales and professional account management.
